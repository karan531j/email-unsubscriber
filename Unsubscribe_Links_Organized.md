## 💡 TIPS & TRICKS

1. **The "Unsubscribe" link must be present by law** (CAN-SPAM Act, GDPR) - it's always there
2. **Most unsubscribes are instant** - you won't receive emails immediately
3. **Some take 24-48 hours** to process (rarely)
4. **Gmail has built-in unsubscribe** - right-click sender → "Unsubscribe"
5. **Don't mark as spam** - just unsubscribe to avoid flagging legitimate senders
